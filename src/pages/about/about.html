About EVAssistant

EV Assistant

EVAssistant is a smart way of dealing with the problems faced by electric vehicles in case of no or low power. The assistant provides three economical solutions for above mentioned problem to the owner of the Electric Vehicle.

  • First, it provides a technical solution by which one can find the nearest charging or swapping station based on the factors mentioned in the problem statement.
  • Second, It focuses on building a platform where the owner of Electric Vehicle in case of no power can contact to the people who are willing to provide power from their vehicle or swap the battery to them by taking reasonable amount of money (these can be small companies or individuals). Also, this could be a source of livelihood for some people. We are planning to set up a rating system for the people so that fraud can be controlled.
  • Third, if above mentioned facilities are not available, the person can call nearby mobile charging stations(a bike carrying battery).
